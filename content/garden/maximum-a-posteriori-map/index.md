## TLDR {#tldr}

No not map, MAP. Look math is hard, naming things is hard it is called MAP leave me alone.

MAP is maximum a posteriori estimation, in <a href="/garden/bayesian-statistics/">Bayesian Statistics</a>. It is the maximum value of a distribution given observation. I.e. it can tell you an estimate of the most likely outcome under the current model given previous observations. MAP is one of the most used Inference Queries in <a href="/garden/bayesian-statistics/">Bayesian Statistics</a>.
